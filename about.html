Sandaruwan Computer

Ratnapura PC Store • Computer Parts • PC Builds

Sandaruwan Computer is a Ratnapura-based computer store focused on reliable PC builds, computer parts, upgrades, repairs and accessories. We help customers choose the right processor, motherboard, RAM, storage and power supply combination for a balanced and compatible computer build.

Our online quotation system is designed to make PC buying easier. Customers can select a processor and automatically view matching motherboard and RAM options, download a branded quotation PDF, add products to cart and place an order through WhatsApp with bank payment details.

We aim to provide clear guidance, fair pricing, fast communication and practical support for office PCs, home computers, study setups, gaming starter builds and custom upgrade packages.
